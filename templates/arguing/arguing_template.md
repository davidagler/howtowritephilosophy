## Argument 1

If people are mean and violent, then God does not exist. People are mean. People are violent. Therefore, God does not exist.

## Argument 2

If something comes into existence, then it must have come from something greater. Things do come into existence. Therefore, there must exist something greater.

## Argument 3

Some basketball players are millionaires. Some millionaires drive fancy cars. Therefore, some basketball players drive fancy cars.
